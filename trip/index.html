The route

A journey sequenced for atmosphere

The trip begins with cultural immersion in Oaxaca, shifts briefly into the Sierra Norte for contrast and stillness, then opens out into a long, restorative coast finish.

Days 1–4

Oaxaca City

Markets, architecture, food, maker visits, mezcal, and the feeling of being properly inside the place rather than just passing through it.

Day 5

Sierra Norte

One night of mountain air and a guided walk: enough to reset the tempo without turning the middle of the trip into a logistical project.

Days 6–11

Pacific Coast

A smaller, design-led coast stay with room for beach time, optional nature, late lunches, and the kind of ending clients remember most fondly.

Why this works

It’s not luxury by volume. It’s luxury by judgement.

Fewer bases, better rhythm, stronger local texture, and enough free space that the trip still feels like theirs rather than a performance of sightseeing.

Chosen stays

Three beautiful bases, each doing a different job

27–30 Nov

Oaxaca courtyard hotel

A calm, design-aware city base with a strong sense of place and easy access to markets, galleries, and evening meals on foot.

30 Nov–1 Dec

Sierra Norte lodge

Simple, scenic, and chosen for the reset it creates in the narrative of the trip — mountain air, a guide, and a quieter night.

1–6 Dec

Pacific coast retreat

Intimate, warm, and visually calm — the kind of place where the final days can hold both planned moments and total idleness without wasting the trip.
